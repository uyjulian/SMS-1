A.8.  What types of of writeable media (DVD-R/DVD+R/DVD+RW/DVD-RW/CDR/CDRW) will work with SMS?

Users have had success with all current types of writeable disc, but the most popular in a recent survey was the DVD-R. Please note that without a mod chip, the only way you can trick the PS2 into reading your home-burned discs, is either the CDDAFS CD-R method or the DVD-R 'Dummy DVD Video Disc' method.
