A.7.  Does the PS2 optical digital output (S/PDIF) work in SMS for AC3 passthrough?

Yes, you can connect the PS2 output to an external audio processor via a fibre optic cable. You can enable the output by selecting SMS Menu -> Player settings. This has been tested with a SoundBlaster PC sound card and 384kb/s ac3 streams.

Note

It is not possible to adjust the audio volume in this mode.
