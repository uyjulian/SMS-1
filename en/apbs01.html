B.1.  VESA Video Modes

Two additional video modes are available for SMS:

  • 640x480@60Hz (press select+L1 during startup)

  • 640x480@75Hz (press select+L2 during startup)

These modes are not accessible in the normal SMS configuration menu - they can only be set at startup.

Note

These have only been tested using a viewsonic VX910 TFT monitor with the official Sony cable.
