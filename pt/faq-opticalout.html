A.7. A saída óptica digital (S/PDIF) da PS2 funciona no SMS para som AC3?

#Yes, you can connect the PS2 output to an external audio processor via a fibre optic cable. This has been tested with a SoundBlaster PC sound card and 384kb/s ac3 streams.#

Nota

#It is not possible to adjust the audio volume in this mode.#
